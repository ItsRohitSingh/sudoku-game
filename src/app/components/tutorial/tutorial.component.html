How to Play Sudoku

The Grid

Sudoku is played on a grid of 9 x 9 spaces. Within the rows and columns are 9 "squares" (made up of 3 x 3 spaces).

The Rules

Each row, column and square (9 spaces each) needs to be filled out with the numbers 1-9, without repeating any numbers within the row, column or square.

Controls

Click a cell, then use your keyboard (1-9) or the on-screen pinpad to enter numbers. Use the arrow keys to navigate. Press Delete or Backspace to erase.
